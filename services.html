Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use a variety of modern technologies depending on project requirements, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue, Angular), PHP (Laravel), Python (Django), and various CMS platforms like WordPress and Shopify. We always choose the best technology stack for your specific needs.

How long does a typical website project take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple brochure website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites or web applications can take 8-12 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support.

Can you help with domain registration and hosting?

Absolutely! We can handle all aspects of your online presence, including domain registration, secure hosting setup, email configuration, and SSL certificates. We offer reliable hosting solutions or can help you choose the best provider for your needs.

What's included in your SEO services?

Our comprehensive SEO services include keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO audits, content strategy, link building, local SEO (if applicable), and monthly performance reporting. We tailor our approach based on your industry and competition.

How do you ensure the security of our website?

We implement multiple security measures including secure coding practices, regular software updates, SSL encryption, firewalls, malware scanning, and secure hosting environments. For e-commerce sites, we ensure PCI compliance and secure payment processing.
