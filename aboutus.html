Our Story

Founded in 2019, Myanmar Coding began as a small team of passionate developers with a shared vision: to create exceptional digital experiences that drive business growth.

What started as a modest operation has grown into one of Myanmar's leading web development agencies, serving clients across industries and borders. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we're proud to have a diverse team of talented professionals who bring creativity, technical expertise, and strategic thinking to every project we undertake.

Our Mission

We exist to empower businesses through innovative digital solutions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and business objectives, creating websites and applications that not only look stunning but deliver measurable results.

We believe in the transformative power of well-executed digital strategies. Whether you're a startup looking to establish your online presence or an enterprise seeking to optimize your digital assets, we're here to help you navigate the digital landscape with confidence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the catalyst for digital transformation in Myanmar and beyond. We aim to set the standard for excellence in web development while fostering a culture of innovation and continuous improvement.

As technology evolves, we're committed to staying at the forefront, ensuring our clients always have access to cutting-edge solutions that give them a competitive edge. We envision a future where every business, regardless of size, can leverage technology to achieve its full potential.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's history

2019

Company Founded

Myanmar Coding was established with a small team of 2 developers working from home.

2020

First Major Client

We landed our first enterprise client, marking our transition from small projects to larger-scale web development.

2021

Team Expansion

Our team grew to 60 members as we added designers, marketers, and project managers to our core development team.

2022

New Office

We moved into our current headquarters in downtown Yangon, with dedicated spaces for each department.

2023

International Projects

We began working with clients outside Myanmar, expanding our reach to Southeast Asia and beyond.

2024

Established 5 more offices

Opened five additional offices across Nay Pyi Taw, Yangon, and Mandalay.
