Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our consultation process

How soon can I schedule a consultation?

We typically schedule consultations within 1-2 business days of your request. During peak times, it may take slightly longer, but we'll do our best to accommodate your preferred timing.

What information should I prepare for the consultation?

It's helpful to have a clear idea of your project goals, target audience, budget range, and timeline. If you have any existing websites or competitors you admire, those references can be valuable too.

Is the consultation really free with no obligation?

Absolutely! Our consultation is completely free with no obligation to proceed with our services. We want you to feel comfortable and informed before making any decisions about working with us.

How long does a consultation typically last?

Most consultations last between 30-45 minutes, depending on the complexity of your project and how many questions you have. We'll make sure to allocate enough time to cover everything important.

What happens after the consultation?

After our call, we'll send you a follow-up email summarizing our discussion along with any next steps. If you decide to proceed, we'll prepare a detailed proposal with timelines and pricing.
